Payout Trainer - Help

This applicaion is designed to help you learn, practice and test on different types of roulette payouts.

  • Straight Up: Pays 35, single number
  • Split: Pays 17, two numbers
  • Corner: Pays 8, 4 numbers
  • Street: Pays 11, 3 numbers
  • Six Line: Pays 5, 6 numbers

Hint: Try the different options above to see where the chips are placed!
